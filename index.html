About Me

Elizabeth holds a certificate from the University of California - Irvine's Coding Boot Camp, received in July, 2018. She is confident with HTML, Javascript, jQuery, and CSS, and using React, Node, SQL, MongoDb and Firebase. She has some knowledge of C#.

She has a master’s degree from Yale University, in European and Russian Studies (focus: Eastern Europe and Russia), and a second master's from Columbia University, in Sociocultural Anthropology. Her undergraduate degree is in International Studies, from the University of Nebraska - Omaha.

In addition to English, Elizabeth speaks French and Russian and she is teaching herself Japanese. She loves puzzles, travel, true crime, video games, and dogs.
